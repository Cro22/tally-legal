What Tally does

Zero-based budgeting

Assign every dollar to a category until "Available to Assign" reaches zero. The YNAB method, built in.

Multi-currency

USD as your base, with JPY, EUR, GBP, MXN, VES and crypto (BTC, USDT, USDC). Live rates, frozen per transaction.

Local-first & offline

Your data lives on your phone and works without a connection. It syncs securely when you're back online.

Private by design

Face ID / Touch ID app lock, a 6-digit PIN fallback, and a private mode that hides your balances at a glance.

Accounts & transfers

Cash, debit, credit and savings accounts. Transfers between them, including cross-currency with optional fees.

Reports that matter

Spending by category, monthly trends, net worth over time, a spending heatmap and an end-of-month forecast.
