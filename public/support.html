Frequently asked questions

Is my financial data private?

Yes. Tally is local-first: your data lives on your device and works fully offline. It syncs securely to your private account so you can restore it, and it is never sold or shared. You can also enable Face ID / Touch ID, a 6-digit PIN, and a private mode that hides balances.

Which currencies does Tally support?

USD is the base currency, with support for JPY, EUR, GBP, MXN, the Venezuelan bolívar (VES, at the official BCV rate), and crypto (BTC, USDT, USDC). Exchange rates are fetched automatically and frozen on each transaction so your history never shifts.

How do I back up or move my data?

Open Settings → Backup to export a full JSON backup, or export your transactions as CSV. You can restore a JSON backup on the same or a new device.

What is zero-based budgeting?

Every dollar of income gets assigned to a category until nothing is left unassigned. Tally's "Available to Assign" shows what's still waiting for a job each month, with rollover between months.

I forgot my password. What now?

Use the password reset link on the sign-in screen, or email us and we'll help you recover access.
